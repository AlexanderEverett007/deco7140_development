Welcome to MindSpace, a student-driven wellness platform designed to help you manage academic pressure, boost mental clarity, and stay connected.

We are a group of passionate students from The University of Queensland, working on creating a supportive space for university students through useful resources, discussion forums, and wellness tips.

Our mission is to make mental health resources more accessible and reduce the stigma around asking for help.

We respect your privacy. All data collected is used solely for improving your wellness experience and is never shared with third parties.
