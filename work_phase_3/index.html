About This Site

This website provides users with access to mental health resources, including information, support tools, and emotional wellness services.

The community focus area for this project is mental health awareness and support. The goal is to provide users with a calm, secure, and user-friendly experience where they can: book appointments, receive AI chatbot guidance, read daily mental wellness quotes, and share experiences with others.

This page represents the foundation of the final product that will help reduce stress and create an inclusive, non-judgmental space for users seeking mental health resources.
