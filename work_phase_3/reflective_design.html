Accessibility Audit

Issue 1

<figure> element lacks a label

WCAG: 1.1.1 Non-text Content

Affected: <figure class="accordion-figure">

Why it matters: Screen reader users can't interpret the figure without a label.

Fix: Add a <figcaption>, e.g.:
<figcaption>Describes the image</figcaption>

Issue 2

Low colour contrast (4.35:1)

WCAG: 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum)

Affected: Text elements with light backgrounds

Why it matters: Low vision users may not be able to read the text.

Fix: Adjust text colour or background for better contrast.

Issue 3

Scrollable <div> not tabbable

WCAG: 2.1.1 Keyboard

Affected: Scrollable containers with no focusable items

Why it matters: Keyboard users can‚Äôt access the content.

Fix: Add tabindex="0" to scrollable divs.
